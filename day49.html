Day 49 - Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Thursday July 12th, 2001

Today is the day I have to make a decision about going to visit north Ethiopia with Dan. And I decide to do it. What reason to stay in Addis and wait for a VISA that might take weeks to be ready? In fact, I still didn't get a confirmation. I called and checked today as well. Plus Dan feels uncomfortable: he feels like he is wasting too much time, and wants to leave very soon for the trip.

As I still don't feel well, I decide to stay home. Dan meets up with his friends (still haven't quite figured out how he met them) and they go to the Merkato. So I take advantage of the situation and ask him to pick up a brush for my jungle boots. And indeed he does. He picks up this brush for 1 birr (!!) which is all handmade with natural hair, probably from a donkey. Pretty cool brush, and pretty cheap, too! It was a gift from him to me.
