Day 46 - Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Monday July 9th, 2001

Naahh. I decided to stay another day in bed. I am still caughing, although the caugh is getting a little better. Ihave to wait for the Sudan visa approval anyway until tomorrow, so there is no point, really, in going out and doing anything.

I ran into nobody at the cafeteria today. I discovered that they have a chess board, that I could have used yesterday to play wth Afework.

But tomorrow I will get on my feet, call the Embassy, find out about the VISA, go drop off my passport, if necessary, and, especially, go change the money, so I can pay the hotel. I have, in fact, an outstanding balance of some hundred birr.
