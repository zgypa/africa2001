Saturday August 25th, 2001

• Good sandwich @ Aroma. Great ice coffee like a crush.

• Meet Ruthi & Guy's dad thru pics & memorial movie.

• Then we got back to Guy's mum's apartment in Holon, and there was the letter that Corina had sent me. She sent me a picture too, her from behind with another 2 friends looking over the sea. You can't see her face, only her hips, covered by a long red skirt, her favorite one, and a tightish green shirt, which would show her back and shoulders. I couldn't look at it for more than 5-10 seconds at a time. What a wonderful body.

And her letter was wonderful too. She really made me want to see her again. It was a good thing, becuase the letter kept my mind away from Hagar, Guy's siter, since I was sleeping in her bed, in her bedroom, with her teddies all around me, and pictures, and clothes... Well, I must say, from the time I talked to her over the phone, that year that Guy's father died, and then I saw the movies, and I saw her move, I fell in love. That's true. Those lips, and voice, and movement... Golly. But she has a boy friend, and has had one for like 5 years now.

And Guy's mum? Out partying again, I guess. She didn't come home tonite. Guy says she is looking for a partner.
