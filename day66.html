Day 66 - F'nota Salam, Ethiopia

Sunday July 29th, 2001

"Dan yelles to bus driver to get money back, so he could get on the next bus, which appeared to be leaving earlier."

He made the assumption, and thus a mistake, that the long distance buses leave like the minibusses in town. Or rather, he didn't really make a mistake, because that is kind of how they work. But his mistake was actually trying to get on the earlier bus once we had the tickets for the later bus already. Of course the bus driver didn't want to give money back for a sold ticket, so Dan had to yell at him. Until he finally did. But Dan was yelling. And I saw it coming. I don't know why, but I did. Of course, once he went to buy the tickets for the other bus, the bus driver told him it was full. So he had to go back and buy the ticket back from the first guy... Let me tell you, it was something. But he managed...

Then the Government ordered the whole bus station drivers to transfer all their passengers to the new arrived buses. It would then hire all the bus station buses for some weird purpose. So we didn't end up having to be 2 days with people whom he had just yelled at. But in the meanwhile, Dan was already thinking of not leaving today at all. Of just going back home, to the Wutma, and maybe flying. Good thing he didn't follow through with this one. In fact, he was thinking of how he shouldn't have interrupted his Omo trip (he wasted much time this way). He laughed when I said: "OK, it could have been perfect, now instead it's just great." but the rest of the time he was going in some pretty serious phases of self meditation there. All and all I found this behavior to be kinda childish, but I don't know Dan that well to be able to make such a judgement. There could be some different element in the personality that I am not aware of...

Then we stop for lunch at this place in Bahar Dar and order the food and the lady wants the money immediately. Dan tells her that that is not how it's usually done, and she leaves. This is enough for a person to be defined as a "cunt" by Dan, which litterally cracks me up.
I just love it when he says it.

All answers I get don't really mean much: "I just do it becauSe we always have done it like this".

soil in south: rain forest like. in north there is a lot more soil.
