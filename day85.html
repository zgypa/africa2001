We went for a swim and when we came back there was a boy sitting on one of our longchairs. We told him (Guy did in Hebrew) that we were there first, but he didn't give a fuck. Another guy had to propose just to get anotherchari, have the bastard guy move over a little, so we could both fit. Butwhat the fuck? Why should they be so arrogant? I mean, also if I had nothing on that chair, and you take it, if I tell you it was mine, you just trust me and excuse yourself and give it back. Jesus!

• Guy's bag got stolen. I put my hipbag under longchair. They only took his bag because it wae sitting on the chairs. A very stupid move. I was lucky, but i should have left my shit in safe.
