Day 67 - Gondar, Ethiopia

Monday July 30th, 2001

In the beginning its all marvellous but after a while one gets accostumed to the scenery.

Today is day 2 on the Cacciamali IVECO bus. It is pretty fast, but still the roads don't allow it to go to fast. Tarmac is worse than dirt roads, unless they are perfectly maintained. The holes that form on the asphalt.... just terribele.

Wake @ 4:45 bus leaves at 5am. Get to Bahar Dar at 9:15 and to Gondar at 2:45. The second part of the trip, Bahar Dar-Gondar was the most enjoyful, as I was stoned and I had a book to read, the Italo Calvino book. I got very absorbed in it. I almost finished it.

A big rock, like a monolith or a statue, was shooting up against the sky with a horseshoe of land around it, as it was looking down to the valley, in way a king may look at its empire.

Arrive in Gondar and do email. Dan found an internet place, thanks to a guide. I went down there, and it seems that the changes I have made, i.e. transferring the page of Luigi over to brillig, worked perfectly. And smoothly. Could not do email, or log in with ssh, becasue the connection was too slow: it would have taken 2h to download teraterm.

My insect bites still itch tremendously. I am suffering a lot for them. They never stop itching, and there is so many, it's almost impossible not to scratch them. They even scratch themselves, like when you are walking on the bus or so.

It looks liek we will be able to get a bus to Metema tomorrow morning at 6am. The busses suck here, but I must say that I am happy that they at least leave every day.

I picked up a silly book, Tess Stimpson: pole position (38EB); and a candle for 1EB

23:51 at our hotel: 2 shots and girl scream. I wake up, and rationalize danger. Dan didn't do a sound. It seemed like I had to say "what the fuck was that" in order for him to realize. I wonder. So I put my boots on. But it was nothing. I just went back to sleep with my boots on, so I didn't have to worry about escaping. That was enough to put me back to sleep.
