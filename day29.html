The procedure there is operator asisted: you write on a piece of paper the number you want to call, the lady operator make the call and tells you to what cabin she will connect you to.Then you go in the cabin, pick up the phone, and she waits until the other side picks up, then starts here CASIO digital stopwatchfor the duratin of the call.

I called Adam. He didn't seem to be too excited that I called.; I don't konw why. Maybe he was but I could not tell. At any rate, I told him I would have called him from arusha, and I did.

Then I went to exchange money again, to a new place, because I wanted to buy some Ksh (Kenian shiling) and I even decided to go back to the first place, because the rates there were a lot lower.So I did, and on the way back, i met another street guy, that said that his brother worked at Riverside, the shuttle I was planning in taking, that I had heard was very good. And he took me there. I bought a ticket, left my back there, and followed the guy, whose name I forget ( I bleive its Ibrahim), to the internet cafe', where I did some of that.

Ibrahim wated outsie. Then I had something to eat, and Ibrahim started talkig to me about girls and drinking and bars. First he said to pay attentino in Nairobi at bars for people putting stuff in your drinks. He told me the story of the Japanese guy.

The ride to Nairobi started perfectly on time and was pretty smooth. I really enjoyed all the landscapes. But some ladies understood what I was saying or that I did not want and respected that and pulled back smiling. Others did not. but a lot of them had big big peircing or stretching f earlobes.

And I told them I had to drop off some oney and exchange some, so I went and did it, and by the time I was done, they were gone. what a shame. I would have liked to go and do some Nairobi night life. So after hat I really did not do much but talk to Smiley, a auto electrician that works here. He is also the local taxi service.

At somet ime along the night I found my self on a hand made long chair, pondering about how there are different types of learning methods. Or you can learn someting or rather you might need to live to believe, and then once you have experined, then you understand better. adn learn. but you then learn the whole field, not just that one thing. No matter what happens or how often. I think I fall in to the second category.

So I just brushed my teeth, then cleaned some grass from stems and seeds, and smoked a little and went to sleep.
